# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 27, 2026

Welcome to Dayleaf. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our daily planner application.

## 1. Information We Collect

Dayleaf is designed to be minimal and private.

Your daily tasks and preferences are stored locally in your browser using local storage. We do not collect or store this data on our servers.

If you choose to purchase premium features, our third-party payment providers (such as Razorpay or Stripe) may collect billing information (such as your name, billing address, and payment details) to process transactions securely.

## 2. How We Use Information

We only use information to:

* Process payments for premium features
* Provide customer support
* Improve the application experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

## 3. Data Storage

All personal task data remains on your device. Dayleaf does not maintain a backend database for storing user content.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Payments are handled by third-party providers. These providers operate under their own privacy policies, and we do not store or have access to your payment details.

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

Dayleaf does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools.

## 6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the application.
